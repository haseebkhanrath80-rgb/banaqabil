Shape a Bright Tomorrow

Pakistan is a young nation, with 64% of its population under the age of 30. Unfortunately, millions of these young individuals are unable to reach their full potential due to a lack of guidance, resources, and opportunities. With approximately 70 million youth facing significant challenges, unemployment stands as the most pressing issue.

Alkhidmat Foundation launched the Bano Qabil IT Scholarship Program to equip Pakistan's youth with modern IT skills. So far, over 50,000 young individuals have completed IT courses.

Extend your support to the Bano Qabil Program and help build a progressive Pakistan.
